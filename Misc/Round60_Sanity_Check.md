## Background Knowledge (Read This First!)

### What is a Sanity Check?

In CTF competitions, a **Sanity Check** is always the easiest challenge — a freebie. It exists just to confirm that:

- You know how to submit a flag
- You understand the flag format (`ictf{...}`)
- You are actually logged in and participating

No hacking, no tools, no scripts needed. Just copy and paste.

### Why "Round 60"?

ImaginaryCTF runs monthly rounds. Round 60 marks **5 years** of the competition running — that is what the description is celebrating!
