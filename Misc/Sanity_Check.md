## Background Knowledge (Read This First!)

### What is a Sanity Check?

In CTF competitions, a **Sanity Check** (also called a "freebie" or "welcome" challenge) is always the easiest challenge. It exists just to confirm that:

- You know how to submit a flag
- You understand the flag format (`ictf{...}`)
- You are actually logged in and participating

No hacking, no tools, no scripts needed. Just copy and paste.

### What is the flag format?

ImaginaryCTF flags always start with `ictf{` and end with `}`. Everything inside the curly braces is the actual secret.
